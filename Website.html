Types of Meditations

Mindfulness Meditation

Focus on the present moment, breathe deeply, and let go of distractions. This meditation helps improve concentration and reduces stress.

Guided Meditation

Follow a guide through a relaxing journey of visualization. Perfect for beginners and for stress relief.

Transcendental Meditation

A mantra-based meditation that helps you reach a state of restful alertness. Ideal for deep relaxation and energy rejuvenation.
